# Introduction

WarpSpace changes the jumping mechanic. Instead of being teleported to your
waypoint (or partway toward it), you enter a parallel dimension — the **Warp** —
which is a scaled-down version of realspace. You travel through it with your
ship's engines just like normal, but distances are ten times shorter.

This means you can follow or be followed while travelling faster than light.
The core feature is that fast-travel becomes **predictable**: flash raids that
rely on jumping away to hide no longer work, and defending your territory is
meaningfully easier.

The Warp is shared by everyone on the server, so it's not unusual to meet other
players while in transit.
