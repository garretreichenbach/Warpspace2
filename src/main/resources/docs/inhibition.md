# Inhibition

Inhibition has been reworked and enhanced. Your HUD will tell you when an
inhibitor is affecting you, and red icons indicate which direction is blocked
(entering warp, leaving warp, or both).

## How it works

- An active inhibitor prevents ships from **entering its sector** and from
  **leaving via jumpdrive**.
- Slowing down and speed-dropping in warp **pierces** the inhibitor.
- Ships or stations fielding an inhibitor can deny warp transit to ships with
  up to **3× larger reactors** within a **3 sector radius**.
- Inhibitors only work while on a loaded entity.
