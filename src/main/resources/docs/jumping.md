# Jumping

1. Set a **navigation waypoint** with `N`.
2. Activate your **jumpdrive**. After a few seconds you will switch dimensions
   and enter the Warp.
3. Follow your waypoint through the Warp. When you arrive at the projected
   warpspace equivalent of your destination, you will see the notification
   *"dropout point reached"*.
4. Activate your jumpdrive again (or slow below 50 m/s for more than 10
   seconds) to drop back out of Warp.
5. You will re-enter realspace at the corresponding **droppoint** — your
   waypoint rounded to the nearest configured interval. Fly the remaining
   distance in realspace.

## Going faster in Warp

Install a **Warp Speed chamber** (a replacement for vanilla Jump Distance) to
increase your maximum flight speed within warpspace. Higher-level chambers
give larger multipliers; see the Configuration guide for exact values on your
server.
