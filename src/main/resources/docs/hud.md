# HUD

In the bottom-right corner of your screen you will see a white HUD element
that reports your current warp state.

## Dimension indicator

- **Hollow spiral** — you are in realspace
- **Filled spiral, spinning fast** — you are in warpspace

## Jump state

- Small **yellow arrows** blinking — you are currently jumping or dropping

## Inhibition state

- **Red spiral** — your current sector is inhibited
- **Red arrows** — the sector in the other dimension is inhibited
